FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need prior gym experience to join?

Not at all. Every membership includes an onboarding session with a trainer who'll assess your fitness level and build a starting plan around it.

Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Yes. All plans can be paused for up to 30 days per year, and cancellations require just 7 days' notice — no lock-in contracts.

Is nutrition coaching included in every plan?

Gold and Elite members get full nutrition coaching. Silver members can add a nutrition consultation as a one-time add-on.

What are your 24x7 access hours?

Gold and Elite members get app-based entry any time, day or night. Silver members have access during staffed hours, 6 AM – 10 PM.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — every new visitor gets one free trial session, including a facility tour and a short consultation with a trainer.
